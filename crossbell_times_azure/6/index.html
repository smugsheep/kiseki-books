Crossbell Times - Issue 6

[Breaking News] CGF Patrol Attacked!
◆Sudden Firefight Ignites Armored Car◆

A recent report from the CGF reveals that sometime between yesterday afternoon and this morning, the third company was suddenly attacked while patrolling near Mainz. Details are limited; however, there is a high number of confirmed casualties. It is possible that the unknown assailants are now occupying Mainz proper. The perpetrators are armed and dangerous.

Our reporter in the field reported the echo of intermittent gunfire resounding from the area around Mainz. The apparent stalemate continued deep into the night.

◆Identity of the Forces◆

Presently, the identity and objective of the assailants remain a mystery. One can't help but think back to the incident at the trade conference just a short while ago.

A former war correspondent told the Times: 'If these are terrorists, they should be making demands. On the other hand, if the perpetrators are jaegers, they ought to be seeking mira or some other compensation.'

The armed force has yet to issue any demands, and their motives remain ambiguous. Fear and rumors of a possible conspiracy are rife among the Crossbellan people.

[Incident] Transcontinental Railroad Back In Service

Yesterday, a Republic-bound train derailed, and many passengers were d in the resulting crash. The Zemurian Railroad Corporation has finished its investigation of the scene, and the line has returned to normal service. The cause of the accident is thought to be an unidentified monster. The CPD's Special Support Section pursued the creature, but no further details regarding the monster or its whereabouts have been reported since.
